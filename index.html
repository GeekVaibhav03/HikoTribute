The Boomer of E-sports

About the Star

CSGO Career:

  • 1990- Spawned in the world on 6th March in United States of America.
  • 2011- Hiko began playing Counter-Strike 1.6 at a competitive level he played for the team called Temorary
  • 2012- Hiko moved straight over to Counter-Strike Global Offensive and would later on join with Area51 Gaming which will later on get acquied by Complexity Gaming.
  • 2014- Cloud9 picked up Complexity Gaming prior to ESL One: Cologne 2014.
  • 2015- Hiko got an offer from FlipSid3 Tactics from Ukraine at ESWC 2015 and after this he joined Team Liquid.
  • 2016- After s1mple joined the Liquid Roster Hiko led the team to semifinals in MLG Columbus 2016.
  • 2020- Hiko retired from csgo and started playing Valorant Competitively for 100Thieves.
